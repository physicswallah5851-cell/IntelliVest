Smart Spending Tips

50-30-20 Rule

Allocate 50% to needs, 30% to wants, and 20% to savings/investing for optimal financial health.

Review Weekly

Check your budgets every Sunday. Catching overspending early gives you time to course-correct before month-end.

Automate Savings

Set up an auto-debit SIP on salary day. What you don't see, you don't spend. Pay yourself first.
