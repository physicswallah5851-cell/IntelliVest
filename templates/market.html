Bond Basics — Understanding Fixed Income

SGB

Sovereign Gold Bonds are issued by RBI. You earn 2.5% p.a. interest + gold price appreciation. Capital gains are tax-free if held till maturity (8 years). Best way to own gold without storage risk.

G-Sec

Government Securities are long-term debt instruments issued by the Central Government. They carry zero credit risk (sovereign guarantee) and offer periodic coupon payments. Traded on RBI's NDS-OM platform.

T-Bills

Treasury Bills are short-term instruments (91/182/364 days) sold at a discount. They don't pay coupon — your return is the difference between purchase price and face value at maturity. Purest risk-free rate.
